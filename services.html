Protection from the top down

Your roof is your home's first line of defense. Whether you're dealing with an active leak, approaching end-of-life materials, or simply want a professional inspection before a major weather season, our roofing team brings thorough diagnostics and precise craftsmanship to every job.

Restoration

Recovering what matters, completely

Restoration after water, fire, or smoke damage demands more than surface repair—it requires understanding the full extent of structural and material compromise before any work begins. We coordinate every phase of recovery: emergency stabilization, demolition of compromised materials, drying and remediation, structural repair, and full cosmetic restoration. You work with one team from start to finish.

  • Fire and smoke damage repair

Specialty Service Ozone Remediation

Odor elimination at the molecular level

Most contractors can clean a surface. Very few can truly eliminate the odors that penetrate building materials, HVAC systems, and structural cavities. Ozone remediation is that capability—and it's one of the most effective tools available for restoring air quality in a home after a damaging event.

HM Consulting operates professional-grade ozone generation equipment, deployed under controlled conditions with proper safety protocols. This is a specialty service that most residential and restoration contractors simply don't offer.

Inquire About Ozone Treatment

Applications

Smoke & Fire Odor

Penetrates porous materials that surface cleaning can't reach, neutralizing smoke compounds at the molecular level.

Pet Odor

Eliminates deep-set pet odors from subfloor, insulation, and wall cavities that persist after cosmetic repairs.

Mold & Mildew

Used after mold remediation to treat residual musty odors and reduce lingering spore activity in treated areas.

Biohazard Post-Remediation

A critical final step after biohazard cleanup to ensure full odor neutralization before a space is reoccupied.

Water Damage

Addresses the stale, damp odors that remain after structural drying is complete but before finishing work begins.

Pre-Sale Preparation

A discreet, effective option for homeowners wanting to present a fresh, odor-free home before listing.

Ozone treatments are performed with all occupants—including pets and plants—removed from the treatment area. Proper ventilation and re-entry timing are managed by our team.
