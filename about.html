About Us

A different kind of home services firm

We built HM Consulting around a simple idea: homeowners deserve honest advice and deliberate workmanship, not just a crew with a clipboard.

Our Foundation

Built on the conviction that your home deserves better.

HM Consulting was established with a clear purpose: to offer homeowners a level of service that combines genuine technical expertise with the kind of honest, thoughtful counsel you'd expect from a trusted advisor—not just a vendor.

Too often, home service companies approach projects with a predetermined outcome. We approach every engagement the opposite way: listen carefully, assess fully, then recommend only what's actually warranted. That philosophy shapes everything we do, from the smallest repair to a ground-up build.

Our team brings deep experience across roofing, remodeling, restoration, and specialized services like ozone remediation—capabilities that allow us to handle complex, multi-phase projects without hand-offs to unvetted subcontractors.

How We Think

Principles that guide every project

These aren't marketing language. They're the standards our team holds itself to on every job, regardless of scope or budget.

01

Listen Before You Recommend

Every project begins with questions, not proposals. We need to understand your goals, your timeline, and your concerns before we can give you honest guidance on what's actually needed.

02

Find the Root Cause

Surface repairs that ignore underlying conditions are expensive over time. We diagnose before we prescribe, and we tell you what we find—including things you might prefer not to hear, because knowing is always better than not knowing.

03

Recommend What's Warranted

We won't push unnecessary work. Our goal is to give you an accurate picture of what your home needs now, what can wait, and what constitutes a smart investment versus an upsell. Your trust is worth more to us than any single invoice.

04

Execute Without Shortcuts

Our craftsmen follow the same standards on a $500 repair as on a $50,000 renovation. Workmanship isn't situational—it's a professional standard. If a detail isn't right, we address it before the job is closed.

Our Team

Specialists, not generalists wearing every hat.

The breadth of services we offer is possible because our team includes genuine specialists in each discipline—roofing crews with dedicated experience, restoration technicians with remediation training, remodeling tradespeople who have spent careers in their craft.

We also maintain capabilities that most firms don't, including professional ozone remediation equipment and trained operators. When a project calls for that level of treatment, it's available through HM Consulting directly—not through a third-party referral with no quality accountability.

What unifies the team is a shared standard: the work is either done right or it isn't done. There's no middle ground on that.
